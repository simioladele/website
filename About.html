About Us

Welcome to your source of food places nearby!
We started this page one warm spring evening when we were feeling very hungry and were looking all over the internet for food places. We thought to ourselves, 'wouldnt it be easier if we could just have all this information on one website?' And just like that, 'FOOD FINDER' was born.
This website is perfect for uni students as everything is FAST and CONVIENIENT. We are still growing with new restaurants and fast food chains being added every 2 weeks!
